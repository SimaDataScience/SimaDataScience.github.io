Implementing an Autograd Engine and Deep Learning Package

Modern deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch and JAX are able to easily differentiate across arbitrarily complex and computational graphs. This makes it very easy to take such functionalities for granted, and can lead to a perceived gap between the math behind deep learning, and its implementation. Here I, with the help of Andrej Karpathy's video here, build a small neural network framework with an Autograd engine and small neural net module using base python and numpy. This was a guided project, and was an invaluable experience that gave me a much deeper understanding and respect for the incredibly optimized frameworks we use today.

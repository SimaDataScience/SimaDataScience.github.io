Text Generation With Pretrained Networks in Tensorflow

I used to have a love/hate relationship with transfer learning: on one hand it took away the satisfaction of designing your own model architecture from the ground up, but on the other hand the results are undeniable. Now pretrained models are often considered the go to method for the majority of image and language related tasks, and leveraging the strength of these models can be incredibly fun and powerful. The following is a notebook that demonstrates the power of pretrained networks by generating artificial text inspired by the first book in the Twilight series, first using a simple LSTM and then the powerful GPT-3 model.
